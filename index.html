Development Areas

Three technical domains that define every deployed solution.

01

Location algorithms

We create SLAM, sensor-fusion, and route-planning pipelines that allow robots to localize with confidence in dynamic indoor environments. This means dependable navigation, map updates, obstacle awareness, and coordinated movement across fleets.

  • Visual-inertial and LiDAR-based localization
  • Map generation for changing industrial layouts
  • Fleet positioning for AMRs and inspection robots
02

Sensor precision

We focus on calibration quality, timing consistency, and signal integrity so the robot sees the physical world accurately. Better precision translates into safer motion, cleaner data, and more repeatable industrial outcomes.

  • Multi-sensor calibration and synchronization
  • Noise filtering and drift compensation
  • Measurement pipelines for tight-tolerance processes
03

Industrial robotics + AI

We apply machine learning and rule-based intelligence where it improves decisions on the line: inspection, anomaly detection, adaptive automation, and operator-facing insights. The result is robotics software that scales from pilot to production.

  • Vision models for inspection and classification
  • Predictive actions for maintenance and throughput
  • Human-in-the-loop workflows with operational analytics
